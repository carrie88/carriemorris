About Carrie

Carrie has creativity and crafting in the blood. Unusually for a digital designer she has a degree in Fine Art, but dont underestimate her technical capabilities. Shes been honing her digital skills since her early teens, and it shows. Carrie keeps herself busy in the evening helping create mobile games, amongst a whole host of other artistic hobbies, from water colours to needle felting and crochet.
