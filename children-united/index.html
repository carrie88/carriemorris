At Yoomee I worked with Save the Children and First News to create a website aimed at helping Children connect with other Children across the globe. The initial site consisted of a redesign of the landing page, expanding the site to blog and survey for children.

The First news colour palette was limited to two colours. So to bring more variety and excitement to the site I chose strong vibrant colours to compliment the primary colours that already existed.
